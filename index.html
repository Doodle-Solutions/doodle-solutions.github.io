Who Are We?

Awaredia Foundation is a dedicated non-profit organization working towards creating a healthier and more informed India.

Our core mission is to educate individuals about nutrition, food labels, protein consumption, and the impact of food and fitness on overall health. We actively work to debunk myths and taboos surrounding health and nutrition while providing evidence-based knowledge.
